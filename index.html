The Challenge Facing Teachers

Educators in Kazakhstan face unique pressures that impact their wellbeing and effectiveness in the classroom.

60%
of teachers report high levels of stress and burnout
42%
feel isolated without professional support networks
35%
consider leaving the profession within 5 years
50+
hours worked weekly with limited resources

How UstazTilek Helps

Our platform provides comprehensive support tailored to the needs of educators in Kazakhstan.

Psychological Support

Access articles, videos, and anonymous chat with mental health professionals specializing in educator wellbeing.

Mentorship System

Find experienced mentors or become one to share knowledge and guidance with fellow educators.

Community Groups

Join safe discussion groups organized by subject, region, or interest to share experiences and solutions.
